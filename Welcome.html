The Speed Read

The loading page or journey starts with a Speed Read, “Welcome to a New Realm of Financial Understanding”. The Speed Read concept, created by Jaie Locke, is fascinating. It represents your ability to handle the content. Studies show that your brain can pick up words and sentences in phrases even if we multiplied the speed 10x. At The Fitzpatrick Group we believe in education. We believe you can understand your finances, various products, the markets, taxes, fees and costs, and strategy, given the proper time and resources. Too many industries do better when you, the consumer, understand less. Your financial future, and financial services, should not be one of those industries. By changing the color of one letter in the Speed Read, you brain remembers and picks up the additional letters and words. It also retains the information better. At The Fitzpatrick Group, we try to focus on simplicity and retention of key words and concepts as we work with our clients, such as: Volatility, Fees, and Taxes. This, we believe, allows you to develop a lifetime strategy that focuses on simplicity and efficiency. The Speed Read also represents the world we live in. This is an information age, and the information is coming at all of us very quickly. Content or information today must be simplified, direct, and educational, to be retained. The current financial services industry is anything but. The current state of finance is over-complicated, misleading, and relies solely on advisors pretending to be smarter than their clients. We hope the Speed Read gives you a sense of what you are capable of, what you can pick up and retain very quickly.

The Storm

It is with this mindset that the graphics and colors of the website were chosen. The dark back drop and clouds depicts the current state of financial services, dangerous, unclear, ominous… a sense of void amidst the overabundance of products and advisor fees and services. Are you at all clear on how your retirement and/or portfolio will perform? What degree of certainty would you associate with your current plan? The dark clouds on the horizon represent an oncoming Storm. We believe that the financial arena is headed toward a storm of its own. The political arena, legislative risk, increasing tax risk, ever increasing market volatility, globalization, and corruption, constantly challenge and impact the status of your retirement portfolio. While there is no way to eliminate all risk from a strategy, we believe the goal should be to try. The current state of financial affairs exacerbates these risks and does little to protect you from their pitfalls. We hope and trust that the dark backdrop did not deter you from our Content and in a way aligns with some of your concerns and beliefs.
